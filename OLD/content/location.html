Our lab is located at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville in the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology. The nearest airport is Knoxville McGhee Tyson Airport (TYS) and we are at the intersection of I-40 and I-75. We’re about three hours by car from Atlanta and Nashville (the nearest airports with direct flights to many destinations). The nearest Amtrak station is about three hours away by car (Atlanta, GA), and we’re approx. 60 miles away from the Appalachian Trail.
