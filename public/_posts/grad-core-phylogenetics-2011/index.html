Topics: Distance Parsimony Long branch attraction Models Model selection Bayes/Likelihood Slides: PDF Homework: The next assignment should be pretty fun. Comments in ALL CAPS are the sections you should focus on for changing. You’re encouraged to play with this assignment: maybe you want to plot the scores as you search, maybe you want to try different search strategies, etc. Feel free to email me for help or talk to your cohort-mates (remember you can use the blackboard forum, too).
